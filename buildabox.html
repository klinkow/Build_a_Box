Build a Box!

This site will teach you

how to

build your own 3-dimensional box!

The following are examples of different boxes.

The purpose of this website is you to teach you how to build the 3-dimensional kind. You will need a sheet of somewhat thick wood and the ability to cut that wood. (Non-wood materials could be used too). This site will ask you about the thickness of your wood and how large you want the box. Then it will generate individualized instructions on how to build your box. Enjoy!!!
